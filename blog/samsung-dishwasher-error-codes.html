Samsung Dishwasher Error Codes: Troubleshoot & Fix Easily

Samsung dishwashers are efficient, but if an error code appears, troubleshooting can help avoid unnecessary repairs. Below are common Samsung dishwasher error codes and how to fix them.

Common Samsung Dishwasher Error Codes

LC / LE – Leakage Detected

Check for water leaks at hoses and seals.

4C – Water Supply Issue

Inlet valve or water pressure problem.

5C / SC – Drain Error

Dishwasher isn't draining properly.

HE – Heating Issue

Water not heating to required temperature.

7E – Waterwall Spray Arm Error

Obstruction in spray arm movement.
