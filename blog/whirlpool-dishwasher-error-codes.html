Whirlpool Dishwasher Error Codes: Quick Fix Guide

Whirlpool dishwashers are widely used, and understanding error codes can save you from costly repairs. Here are the most common errors and how to resolve them.

Common Whirlpool Dishwasher Error Codes

F2E2 – Control Board Issue

Faulty control panel or wiring.

F7E1 – No Heat Detected

Heating element failure.

F6E4 – Float Switch Activated

Water leak or stuck float.

F8E4 – Water Overflow

Excessive water detected.

F9E1 – Drain Pump Issue

Dishwasher unable to drain properly.
