LG Dishwasher Error Codes & Fixes: Troubleshoot Your Appliance Fast

LG dishwashers are reliable, but like any appliance, they can experience issues. Below are the most common LG dishwasher error codes and how to fix them.

Common LG Dishwasher Error Codes

OE – Drainage Issue

Check for a clogged drain hose or filter.

LE – Motor Error

Motor may be overloaded; try resetting.

HE – Heating Error

Heating element malfunction or water not reaching the correct temperature.

FE – Water Overflow

Excess water detected; faulty inlet valve or stuck float switch.

AE – Leakage Detected

Water leakage in base; inspect hoses and seals.
